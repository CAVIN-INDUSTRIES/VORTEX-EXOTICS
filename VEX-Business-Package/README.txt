WHO THIS IS FOR
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This folder is written so investors, partners, dealers, and curious readers can
follow along without a software or finance background. Where we use a technical
word, we explain it briefly. Deeper detail stays available for specialists.

HOW TO OPEN THESE FILES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  .txt files          Double-click to open in Notepad (Windows) or any text app.
  VEX-Pitch-Deck.pptx Open with Microsoft PowerPoint or LibreOffice Impress.

SUGGESTED READING ORDER
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  1. README.txt (this file) — how the package fits together
  2. 00-Letter-of-Transmittal.txt — formal cover letter and what is inside
  3. 01-VEX-Complete-Business-Overview.txt — what VEX is and how the business works
  4. 02-Financial-Projection-Report.txt — real prices from the product, plus a
     blank worksheet for your finance team (no invented “future revenue” here)
  5. 03-Strategic-Business-Advisor-Report.txt — outside-style strategy summary
  6. NOTE-Pitch-Deck-Numbers.txt — important notes before sharing the slides
  7. VEX-Pitch-Deck.pptx — presentation (dark obsidian / violet–gold theme)

SHARING THE PACKAGE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Zip the whole folder as VEX-Business-Package.zip. Recipients unzip and start
with README.txt.
