IMPORTANT — ABOUT THE NUMBERS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The **Financial Projection Report** (file 02) is honest about what is “real”
versus what is “for your finance team to fill in.”

  REAL (from the actual product today):
    • The **published subscription list prices** customers see in the software
      tier names Starter, Pro, and Enterprise — currently $49, $149, and
      $299 per month (with yearly options). These come from the live product
      configuration (technical path in the code: pricing.ts).

    • A plain-English description of how the system estimates **monthly
      recurring revenue** (MRR) for a dealer account — meaning “how much
      subscription money that one account would represent per month” when
      billing is active.

  NOT IN THIS PACKAGE:
    • Made-up multi-year revenue totals, fake customer counts, or guaranteed
      profit forecasts. Those belong in models your **finance or accounting**
      team builds from **Stripe** (payment system), bank records, and contracts.

  ALSO EXPLAINED:
    • Some screens in the product use **placeholder** numbers for demos (for
      example sample churn or sample company valuation). Those are labeled so
      nobody mistakes them for audited financials.
